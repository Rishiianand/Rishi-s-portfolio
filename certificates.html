Introduction to Artificial Intelligence(AI)

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a course that teaches students about the basics of AI, its applications, and how it is transforming our lives. The course covers AI concepts and terms like machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks. It also explores various issues and concerns surrounding AI such as ethics and bias, and jobs, and provides advice from experts about learning and starting a career in AI.

Programming For Problem Solving

Programming for problem solving is an essential skill for computer programmers. It involves identifying problems and generating creative solutions before developing a program. Problem-solving in programming is the process of breaking down a complex problem into smaller, more manageable parts, and then solving each part systematically
